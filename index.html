Meet our Pastor

Pastor Ebunoluwa Ojo was ordained into the work of the ministry in the year 2017 when he felt the call of God upon his life to reach out to unsaved souls. Through him, God has performed different kinds of signs and wonders in different nations of the world. He is a pharmacist by profession and a graduate of Obafemi Awolowo University.
